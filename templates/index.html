Understanding Heart Disease Through Data

Heart disease is a group of diseases related to cardiovascular diseases, manifested by a violation of the normal functioning of the heart. May be caused by damage to the epicardium, pericardium, myocardium, endocardium, valvular apparatus of the heart, heart vessels.

According to the National Heart, Lung and Blood Institute in Framingham (USA), the most important factors in the development of cardiovascular disease in humans are obesity, sedentary lifestyle and smoking.
